SHARE AND CARE
This website was made to ensure greater sharing and co-ordination among the residents of a hostel particularly. The website contains the details like name, room number, wing number, item to spare(extra item), cost of item, etc. The item is removed once the item is claimed by someone or is not available due to some other reason. The website was made collabratively using git and github by the team members.
